About WASH INNOVATION HUB
WIH is a catalyst for WASH transformation. We foster a collaborative ecosystem that accelerates innovation, empowering cities and communities to address water, sanitation, and hygiene challenges. By nurturing a network of over 800 startups and innovators, we co-create and scale solutions that drive sustainable change. Know More
